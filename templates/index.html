⚙ 10 Physics Consistency Scores

ECS
Energy Conservation Score
Reflected energy ≤ incident energy. Uses GTR VNDF importance sampling for 10× lower Monte Carlo variance.
SCS
Specular Consistency Score
GGX NDF predicts the exact specular highlight shape for a given roughness. Deepfakes get this wrong.
BSS
BRDF Smoothness Score
Real skin material properties vary smoothly in space. Abrupt BRDF jumps indicate artificial generation.
ICS
Illumination Coherence Score
Real faces concentrate lighting energy in SH bands l=0,1. Fakes leak into l=2 (quadratic/noisy terms).
TPS
Temporal Physics Stability
Physics properties must be stable across video frames. Deepfakes show inter-frame physics flickering.
SSS
Subsurface Scattering Score
Skin is translucent — red light scatters deeper than blue, so R > G > B always holds (Jensen dipole model).
FAS
Fresnel Anomaly Score
Skin Fresnel reflectance F₀ ≈ 0.028 (refractive index ~1.4). A hard physical constant deepfakes violate.
WAS
Wasserstein Anomaly Score
Mahalanobis distance from the calibrated distribution of real-face physics. Distribution-level anomaly detection.
ShCS NEW
Shadow Consistency Score
Cast shadows must align with the dominant light direction from SH. Detects faces composited under mismatched lighting.
SpSS NEW
Spectral Skin Score
Face color must be explainable by valid melanin + hemoglobin concentrations via Beer-Lambert spectral absorption.

Architecture Pipeline

  • Face Point Sampler — Learnable depth net lifts 2D → 3D
  • PBR-NeRF Backbone — SDF + Disney BRDF decomposition
  • SH Lighting — 2nd-order Spherical Harmonics (27 params)
  • 10-Score Scorer — ECS, SCS, BSS, ICS, TPS, SSS, FAS, WAS, ShCS, SpSS
  • Wasserstein Detector — Distribution-level anomaly
  • Cross-Attention Classifier — Physics × visual fusion

vs State of the Art

  • XceptionNet: 99.7% FF++ → 65% CelebDF (overfits)
  • Face X-ray: Only detects blending boundaries
  • Light2Lie (NDSS'26): Physics, but only 3 signals
  • M2F2-Det (CVPR'25): CLIP+LLM, good but black box
  • PhysForensics: 10 physics signals, interpretable, generalizable
  • Key advantage: Physics laws don't change when generators do

New Physics Laws (v2 Extended)

  • Shadow geometry: Cast shadows align with SH light direction
  • Spectral skin: Color = melanin + hemoglobin (Beer-Lambert)
  • RGB constraint: Real skin always has R > G > B
  • Skin gamut: Colors must lie within measured skin gamut
  • F₀ = 0.028: Skin Fresnel constant from IOR n=1.4
  • SH band ratio: Real lighting: l0+l1 energy >> l2 energy
