Architecture Components

  • PBR-NeRF Backbone — SDF + Disney BRDF + NeILF inverse rendering
  • Physics Consistency Scorer — 5 physics scores (ECS, SCS, BSS, ICS, TPS)
  • Forensic Classifier — Cross-attention fusion of visual + physics features
  • Face Point Sampler — Learnable depth estimator lifting 2D to 3D
  • CLIP Backbone — Visual feature extraction with gated fusion
  • Advanced Physics (v2) — SH lighting, SSS, GTR BRDF, Wasserstein loss

Physics Laws Enforced

  • Energy Conservation — Reflected light cannot exceed incident light
  • Fresnel Equations — F = F₀ + (1-F₀)(1-cosθ)⁵
  • Microfacet BRDF — Surface roughness determines specular highlight shape
  • Subsurface Scattering — Jensen dipole model for skin translucency
  • Coherent Illumination — Single consistent light field per face
  • Temporal Stability — Physics should be consistent across video frames
